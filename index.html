Safety Guide by Mahmodul Hasan

Section 1: Introduction

  • ✅ Welcome to the safety guide prepared by Mahmodul Hasan.
  • ✅ Covering international standards and practical tips.

Section 2: OSHA Guidelines

  • 📘 Understand OSHA rights and responsibilities.
  • 📘 Ensure proper PPE and hazard training.
  • 📘 Keep logs of injuries (OSHA 300).

Section 3: ISO 45001 Overview

  • 📘 Focuses on Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems.
  • 📘 Risk-based thinking for proactive control.

Section 4: NEBOSH Key Points

  • 📘 International General Certificate is essential for safety officers.
  • 📘 Emphasis on risk assessment and incident investigation.

Section 5: Worksite Inspection

  • 🛠️ Daily walk-throughs to identify hazards.
  • 🛠️ Document findings and take corrective actions.

Section 6: Machine Safety

  • ⚙️ Ensure moving parts are guarded.
  • ⚙️ Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) procedures must be followed.
  • ⚙️ Train operators for emergency shutdown.

Section 7: Chemical Handling

  • ☣️ Read SDS before using any chemical.
  • ☣️ Use chemical-resistant gloves and goggles.

Section 8: Fire Safety

  • 🔥 Fire exits must be clear and marked.
  • 🔥 Conduct fire drills quarterly.

Section 9: Lifting & Rigging

  • 🏗️ Only certified personnel should rig loads.
  • 🏗️ Inspect slings and hooks before use.

Section 10: Scaffolding

  • 🪜 Scaffolds must be inspected daily.
  • 🪜 Guardrails and toeboards are mandatory.

Section 11: Working at Height

  • 🪂 Use full-body harness tied to anchor point.
  • 🪂 Avoid working during high winds.

Section 12: Electrical Safety

  • ⚡ Never touch live wires without proper tools.
  • ⚡ Label circuit breakers and isolate before work.

Section 13: First Aid

  • 🚑 Always have a stocked first-aid kit accessible.
  • 🚑 Train staff in CPR and emergency response.

Section 14: Reporting

  • 📝 Report all near-misses and incidents promptly.
  • 📝 Use forms and logs for documentation.

Section 15: Conclusion

  • ✔️ Safety is everyone's responsibility.
  • ✔️ Stay alert, stay safe.
